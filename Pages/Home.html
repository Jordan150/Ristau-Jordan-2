Welcome to the Asone Region

A fan made pokemon game designed by Truegreen7.

It is based on the Middle east. Aone has many different areas and different ways of life in each. It is the birthplace of pokemon training and is has pokemon that are both very powerful and intelligent.

New battling mechanic! Poketactics!

Add a whole new level to your pokemon battling strategies with Poketactics.

Each Pokemon has two Poketactics that they can use depending on the species. They can be used in battle in addition to regular attacks

Asones Creator

Asone and all of its pokemon, characters, and lore were created by Ron Sroor. All artwork shown here was originally made by him. You can find more information about him and the games on his Youtube channel called Truegreen7. While the Asone region is just a concept he created he went in depth detailing how the games would be and has created many more pokemon than shown here.
