The Legendaries

Shemorera

Shemorera is the box art legendary pokemon for pokemon Amber. It was said to have taught pokemon how to use elemental powers in ancient times. It is partially responsible for the war between humans and Pokemon that occurred long ago.

Azarera

Azarera is the box art legendary pokemon for pokemon Jade. It was said to have taught mankind how to use forage tools necessary to survive in the world of pokemon in ancient times. It is partially responsible for the war between humans and Pokemon that occurred long ago.

Emiseraph

Emiseraph is the legendary pokemon that was the messenger of Arceus. It was sent to quell the conflict between the Shemorera and Azarera before the war, but its true form threw pokemon into a hysteric state upon seeing it, kickstarting the war.

Emiseraph: Hysteria form

Emiseraphs Hysteria form can cause any pokemon who sees it to grow violent and go on rampages. It is not meant for being seen by pokemon and id this legendaries true form.

History of Asone

The Pokemon in Asone have especially high intelligence and Asone is where battaling originated in the Pokemon world. Fear of pokemon and the relationship between humans and Pokemon both play a strong role in the plot as many distrust pokemon due to a large scale war that occurred in the ancient past of Asone, but others see them as companions abe to work alongside them in peace. All three of the main legendary pokemon in these games were involved in the war in some way.

Team Ozone

Team Ozone is the evil team in the Asone region. They present themselves to the public with the cover of trying to protect people from dangerous pokemon, however they also steal pokemon from trainers claiming they are too dangerous for them to handle. They are seen as heroic or villainous as a team. While they do try to protect people from powerful pokemon attacks, they also cause conflict in the region by making people fear pokemon and send Pokemon on rampages. Captain Ozone is a prominent member of the team and is featured in team Ozones tv broadcasts frequency. While he is on the evil team, as he learns more about the team's real motives he tries to redeem himself and helps the player. Your father is the leader of team Ozone.

Gym leaders/people

The Gym leaders of Asone are all important figures in Asone and have differing ideologies and views. They serve as challenging opponents for the player to face on their journey to become champion and they will teach you how to utilize more poke tactics the more of them that you defeat. There are eight gym leaders in Asone that you must defeat to become eligible for becoming champion. The first gym leader you will face is Daniel

Professor Cedar is a famous Pokemon professor in Asone who gives you your starter Pokemon. In this game your rival is the trainer you didn't choose to be and is the child of the professor.

On your Journey you will meet another trainer and rival named Evan. After saving his Pokemon from team Ozone he sees you as a worthy adversary and friend.
