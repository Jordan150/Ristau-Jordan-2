Team Ozone

Team Ozone is the evel team in the Asone region. They presents themselves to the public with the cover of trying to protect people from dangerous pokemon, however they also steal pokemon from trainers claiming they are too dangerous for them to handel. They are seen as heroic or villonous as a team. While they do try to protect people from powerful pokemon attacks, they also cause conflict in the region by making people fear pokemon and sent Pokemon on rampages.
